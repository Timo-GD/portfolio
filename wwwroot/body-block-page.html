Hold the Line

Body block is a p5.js platformer. It was made together with Samuel Zwijsen.

In the project I worked on the movement, collision and the body dropping and movement of the dropped bodies.
The most difficult part of this project was the movement and the collision script.
In the movement script the biggest challenge was the vertical movement and making it feel good for a platformer. To fix the vertical movement we used jump buffers and coyote time. We also used deceleration for the horizontal movement which helped with making the movement feel great.
The collision was a whole different problem. We eventually made a system that checked the closest tiles to the player and handled the collision of those tiles.
